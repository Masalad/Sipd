How the community works
1
Pay R 5 once and join
A once-off R 5 joining fee via PayFast verifies you as a real SA member, assigns your FIFO queue position, and activates automatic PayFast payouts to your linked bank account. You are never charged again.
2
System assigns your recipient
You do not choose who you send to. Sipd automatically selects the next eligible person — the member who has been waiting the longest in the queue and still has open receive slots. This is FIFO (First In, First Out) and ensures complete fairness.
3
Gift at your own pace — 1, 2, or all 3 cups
There is no pressure to send all 3 cups at once. You can send just 1 or 2 if you prefer. The options are Small (R 55), Medium (R 90), and Large (R 165) — one of each size per day, up to 3 total. It's entirely your choice how many you send each day.
4
Each cup puts you in line for 2 potential gifters
Every cup you send places you in the queue to potentially receive 2 gifts back from other community members. The more you send, the more gifters you could attract — up to 6 per day if all 3 cups are sent. Receiving is not guaranteed but depends on community activity.
5
PayFast pays you automatically
When someone sends you a cup, the payment goes directly to your linked SA bank account via PayFast Split Pay. No manual confirmation needed, no delays, no holding of funds.
